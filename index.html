Research

I work on developing computational genomic methods.

The drop in sequencing costs has led to an explosion of publicly available data. With this rapid growth of public sequencing data, it has become more difficult for researchers to utilize all the available information without incurring a large cost due to processing time or memory usage. My work looks to leverage the rapidly growing number of references in public databases such as NCBI RefSeq by applying pan-genomic methods, and data-structures.
